About CKD

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) affects kidney filtering over time and may lead to serious health issues. This platform integrates AI prediction with specialist consultation and care planning for early intervention.

Use the full cycle: predict your risk, track trends with data, and connect with nephrology experts for personalized treatment guidance.

How to Use

  • Run the CKD Care prediction test for a risk profile.
  • Review your prediction history and ongoing trend analytics.
  • Book a doctor consultation directly from this dashboard.
  • Complete online payment (for approved sessions) and receive secure e-consult link.
  • Follow personalized recommendations from your health report.
